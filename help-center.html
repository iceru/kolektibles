An account ban refers to the permanent disabling of a user's account on Kolektibles platform, typically as a result of violating the platform's terms of service or community guidelines. A suspended account is a temporary measure where the account is disabled for a certain period of time, usually as a warning or a punishment for a less severe infraction. Both banned and suspended accounts are unable to access the platform or interact with other users until the ban or suspension is lifted.

To solve an account hack by an unknown person on a platform, the first step is to immediately change your password. Next, check for any suspicious activity or unauthorized purchases on the account.
